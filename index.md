### Introduction
I am a Master's student at the **KAIST Graduate School of Culture Technology**, specializing in **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)** and **Computer Vision** for games. I am fortunate to be advised by **Prof. Young Yim Doh**. My work explores how AI can bridge the gap between complex game systems and diverse player abilities.

### Research Interests
* **Game Accessibility:** Designing adaptive AI assistants for players with disabilities.
* **Generative AI in Games:** Utilizing LLMs to enhance NPC agency and interactive narratives.

  <p>The era of "one-size-fits-all" game design is ending. With the emergence of Generative AI, we now have the tools to make games truly personal. I believe the future of game accessibility isn't just about "fixing" barriers, but about <b>empowering players</b> to redefine how they interact with virtual worlds. My goal is to build the intelligent interfaces that make this possible.</p>
